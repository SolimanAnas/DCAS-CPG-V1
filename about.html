The Initiative

As a member of the CPG team, I took the initiative to develop this digital platform.

While working on the guidelines, I recognized the need to bridge the gap between our comprehensive static documents and the modern, on-the-go needs of field crews. My goal was to transform our protocols into a dynamic, interactive learning experience that fits in your pocket.
